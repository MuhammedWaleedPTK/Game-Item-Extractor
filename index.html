Mastering the Settings

Items to Extract: Auto-detect finds separate objects, or you can force a specific count.
Remove Background: Toggle the AI engine. Works best on images with clear subjects.
Quality: Pixel is crisp/original. Smooth/Ultra uses AI to clean edges.
Drop Shadow: Add depth with customizable color, opacity, distance, and spread.
Outline: Add a colored border around items with adjustable thickness and fade.
Padding: Add breathing room around your items before exporting.
Export Size: Choose Exact Fit or scale to standard resolutions (256, 512, etc).
File Prefix: Set a custom name (e.g., Sprite_) for your exported files.
